Quack Control — Privacy Policy

Last updated: 3 June 2026

Short version: Quack Control collects nothing about you. No accounts, no analytics, no ads, no tracking. The game works fully offline; the only time it touches the network is if you choose to make the optional one-time purchase, which is handled entirely by Google Play.

Data we collect

None. Quack Control does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal or sensitive information. We do not use accounts, advertising, analytics, crash reporting, cookies, location, device identifiers, or any tracking technology.

Data stored on your device

The game saves a small amount of information only on your device so it can remember your progress: which levels and ponds you have cleared, your best scores and stars, your golden-egg balance and unlocks, whether you own the optional full-game unlock, and your settings (sound, music, vibration, and language). This never leaves your phone, is never sent to us or anyone else, and is removed if you uninstall the app or use Reset Data in the in-game Settings.

Purchases

Quack Control offers a single optional one-time in-app purchase that unlocks the full game. If you choose to buy it, the purchase is processed entirely by Google Play Billing. We never see or receive your name, email, or payment details — Google handles the transaction and only tells the app whether the unlock is owned. Google's handling of that purchase is covered by Google's Privacy Policy. The app is free and the purchase is never required.

Network & permissions

Apart from Google Play Billing for the optional purchase above, Quack Control makes no network requests. It requests no runtime device permissions — no location, contacts, camera, microphone, storage, or advertising identifiers. The game is fully playable offline.

Third parties

The app contains no third-party advertising networks, analytics providers, or tracking SDKs. The only third-party service is Google Play Billing, used solely to process the optional purchase. Because we collect nothing, nothing is shared or sold.

Children & families

Quack Control is a gentle, non-violent game suitable for all ages, including children, and we have designed it to comply with the Google Play Families Policy and applicable children's privacy laws, including the U.S. Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and the EU General Data Protection Regulation, including its provisions for children (GDPR‑K).

Specifically, for all users — and children in particular — Quack Control:

  • collects no personal or sensitive information from anyone, and therefore never knowingly collects any from a child;
  • does not access precise location and does not transmit any device or advertising identifiers;
  • shows no advertising of any kind, and therefore no personalized or behavioral ads to children;
  • includes no third-party SDKs or APIs that collect data (the only third party, Google Play Billing, processes the optional purchase and receives no data from the app);
  • keeps all gameplay data on the device, where it can be deleted at any time by uninstalling the app or using Reset Data in Settings.

Because the app collects no data from anyone, no verifiable parental consent is required for any data collection. If you are a parent or guardian and have any questions, please contact us using the email below.

Changes

If this policy ever changes, the updated version will be posted here with a new date. Since the app collects no data, material changes are unlikely.

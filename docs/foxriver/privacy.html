Fox River — Privacy Policy

Last updated: 30 May 2026

Short version: Fox River collects nothing. No accounts, no analytics, no ads, no tracking, and no internet connection. Everything stays on your device.

Data we collect

None. Fox River does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal information. We do not use accounts, advertising, analytics, crash reporting, cookies, or any tracking technology.

Data stored on your device

The game saves a small amount of information only on your device so it can remember where you left off: your current level, best score, and your sound on/off preference. This never leaves your phone, is never sent anywhere, and is removed if you uninstall the app or use Reset Data in the in-game Settings.

Network & permissions

Fox River makes no network requests and requests no device permissions (no internet, location, contacts, camera, storage, or identifiers). The app works fully offline.

Third parties

There are no third-party services, SDKs, ad networks, or analytics providers in the app. Because nothing is collected, nothing is shared or sold.

Children

Fox River is suitable for all ages and collects no data from anyone, including children.

Changes

If this policy ever changes, the updated version will be posted here with a new date. Since the app collects no data, material changes are unlikely.

Contact

Questions about this policy? Contact the developer at boredpotatoapps@gmail.com.

Fox River is a free, offline, single-player puzzle game. This page is the privacy policy referenced by its Google Play listing.
